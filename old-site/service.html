Construction and Civil Engineering

Road Maintenance, Safety Management and Road Construction

    Road Construction & Maintenance Services
  • Surface Treatment
    Specialized services include asphalt paving, resurfacing, and road surface treatments.
  • Civil Works
    General road construction, including tar road maintenance and repair.
  • Preventive Maintenance
    Routine inspections, repairing cracks, and pothole filling to extend road life.
  • Drainage & Vegetation Control
    Maintaining proper drainage systems and managing roadside vegetation to prevent damage.


    • Road Safety Management
  • Work Zone Safety
    Utilizing bright orange cones, barrels, and concrete median barriers to separate traffic from workers.
  • Traffic Control
    Implementing modern flagging operations, reduced speed limits, and signage to guide traffic safely around work sites.
  • Worker Protection
    Enforcing the use of personal protective equipment (PPE), including high-visibility clothing, hard hats, and steel-toed shoes.
  • Audits and Inspections
    Conducting road safety audits (RSA) and inspections to identify and treat potential hazards (blackspots).
Mechanical Engineering

Design, development, and maintenance of physical systems and machinery

  • Design and Development: Creating 3D models, mechanical drawings, and data sheets for new components or systems.
  • Manufacturing and Production: Managing the fabrication and assembly of machines, including material selection and tooling.
  • Maintenance and Reliability: Providing troubleshooting, equipment rebuilding, and scheduled repairs to ensure long-term operational efficiency.
  • System Analysis:Conducting stress analysis, fluid dynamics testing, and thermodynamic evaluations to optimise performance.
  • Installation and Commissioning: Ensuring equipment is properly set up and functioning according to strict technical specifications.
Cleaning and Waste Management

Cleaning Services

  • Commercial & Contract Cleaning: Daily or scheduled maintenance for offices, retail spaces, and public buildings.
  • Industrial Cleaning: Specialized cleaning for factories and plants, including high-pressure (HP) cleaning, tank cleaning, and industrial vacuuming.
  • Hygiene & Sanitation: Management of washroom dispensers, sanitary bin services, and the supply of hygiene consumables like toilet paper and handsoaps.
  • Specialised Cleaning: Deep cleaning for high-level areas (silos, vents), carpet and upholstery care, and window cleaning.
  • Hazardous Materials (Hazmat) Cleaning: Safe removal of contaminated materials, asbestos stripping, and incident scene management.


Waste Management Services

  • Collection & Transportation: Regular door-to-door or onsite collection of domestic, commercial, and industrial waste.
  • Recycling & Sorting: Onsite or offsite separation of recyclable streams like paper, plastic, glass, and metal to reduce landfill dependency.
  • Hazardous Waste Management: Secure handling, treatment, and disposal of toxic, medical, and chemical wastes.
  • Organic Waste & Composting: Processing food and garden waste into compost or animal feed.
  • Landfill Management: Planning, construction, and operation of landfill sites for non-recyclable waste.
