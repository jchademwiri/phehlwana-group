## Service 1 — Construction and Civil Engineering

**Icon:** `fa-hard-hat` or `fa-building` *(replace the hospital icon)*

**Image:** `public/images/services/construction.jpg`
**Alt text:** "Phehlwana Group construction team working on a building project in Pretoria"

**Heading:** Construction and Civil Engineering

**Summary:** From new builds and renovations to road construction and safety management — we deliver quality civil and building work to CIDB standards.

---

### 1A — General Building Construction and Maintenance

#### New Builds
Constructing houses, offices, factories, schools, clinics, and other commercial and
public facilities. We manage the full build process from foundation to final handover.

#### Renovations & Remodelling
Updating and transforming existing spaces — kitchens, bathrooms, offices, and entire
buildings. We handle structural changes including adding or removing walls, partitions,
and mezzanine floors.

#### Structural Work
Foundations, brickwork, concrete work, steel installations, and structural repairs.
All structural work is carried out in compliance with SANS standards and relevant
building regulations.

#### Finishes & Interiors
Painting, tiling, ceilings, plastering, drywalling, built-in cupboards, and complete
internal fit-outs. We work with clients to achieve the finish and aesthetic they envision.

#### Specialised Trades
Electrical installations, plumbing, drainage, waterproofing, and carpentry — all
carried out by qualified tradespeople.

#### External Works
Roofing, gutter installation, paving, landscaping, and retaining walls. We handle
the full exterior of your building.

#### Maintenance & Repairs
Planned and reactive maintenance including roof repairs, plumbing emergencies, general
upkeep, and facility management. Regular maintenance prevents costly breakdowns and
extends the life of your building.

---

### 1B — Road Construction, Maintenance & Safety Management

#### Road Construction & Maintenance

**Surface Treatment**
Asphalt paving, resurfacing, and road surface treatments to extend road life and
improve safety for road users.

**Civil Works**
General road construction including tar road maintenance, repair, and new road
development for municipalities and private clients.

**Preventive Maintenance**
Routine road inspections, crack sealing, and pothole filling to address issues before
they become costly failures.

**Drainage & Vegetation Control**
Maintaining proper stormwater drainage systems and managing roadside vegetation to
prevent road damage and improve visibility.

#### Road Safety Management

**Work Zone Safety**
Deploying traffic management equipment including cones, barrels, and concrete median
barriers to safely separate road users from active work zones.

**Traffic Control**
Implementing flagging operations, temporary speed limit signage, and traffic flow
management to guide road users safely around construction sites.

**Worker Protection**
Enforcing the use of personal protective equipment (PPE) on all road sites — including
high-visibility clothing, hard hats, and steel-toed boots — in compliance with the
OHS Act.

**Road Safety Audits & Inspections**
Conducting formal road safety audits (RSA) and site inspections to identify and
remediate potential hazards and blackspots before they cause incidents.

## Service 2 — Mechanical Engineering

**Icon:** `fa-cogs` or `fa-tools`

**Image:** `public/images/services/mechanical.jpg`
**Alt text:** "Mechanical engineer inspecting industrial machinery"

**Heading:** Mechanical Engineering

**Summary:** Design, development, installation, and maintenance of mechanical systems
and machinery for industrial, commercial, and municipal clients.

---

### Design and Development
Creating detailed 3D models, mechanical drawings, and technical data sheets for new
components, systems, and machinery. Our engineers work closely with clients to develop
solutions that meet exact operational requirements.

### Manufacturing and Production
Managing the fabrication and assembly of mechanical components and machines, including
material selection, tooling, and quality control throughout the manufacturing process.

### Maintenance and Reliability
Providing scheduled preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, equipment rebuilding, and
emergency repairs to ensure long-term operational efficiency and minimise downtime.

### System Analysis
Conducting stress analysis, fluid dynamics testing, and thermodynamic evaluations to
optimise the performance and longevity of mechanical systems.

### Installation and Commissioning
Ensuring all equipment is correctly installed, calibrated, and commissioned according
to strict technical specifications and manufacturer requirements before handover.

### Safety Management
All mechanical engineering work is carried out in full compliance with the Occupational
Health and Safety Act (OHS Act 85 of 1993). Our engineers conduct risk assessments and
implement safe work procedures on every project.

## Service 3 — Cleaning and Waste Management

**Icon:** `fa-broom` or `fa-recycle`

**Image:** `public/images/services/cleaning.jpg`
**Alt text:** "Phehlwana Group cleaning team in a commercial office building"

**Heading:** Cleaning and Waste Management

**Summary:** From daily commercial cleaning to hazardous waste disposal — we deliver
hygienic, compliant, and environmentally responsible solutions.

---

### Cleaning Services

#### Commercial & Contract Cleaning
Daily, weekly, or scheduled cleaning maintenance for offices, retail spaces, shopping
centres, schools, clinics, and public buildings. We provide dedicated cleaning teams
on flexible contracts tailored to your operational needs.

#### Industrial Cleaning
Specialised cleaning for factories, warehouses, and processing plants — including
high-pressure (HP) cleaning, tank cleaning, industrial vacuuming, and degreasing.

#### Hygiene & Sanitation
Full management of workplace hygiene including washroom dispensers, sanitary bin
services, and the supply of hygiene consumables (toilet paper, hand soap, sanitiser).

#### Specialised Cleaning
Deep cleaning for hard-to-reach areas including silos, ventilation systems, and
elevated surfaces. Also includes carpet and upholstery cleaning, window cleaning,
and post-construction clean-ups.

#### Hazardous Materials (Hazmat) Cleaning
Safe removal and disposal of contaminated materials, asbestos stripping, and incident
scene management — carried out by trained personnel with the correct PPE and in
compliance with NEM:WA regulations.

---

### Waste Management Services

#### Collection & Transportation
Regular scheduled collection of domestic, commercial, and industrial waste — including
door-to-door and onsite collection services. All waste is transported in compliant
vehicles with proper waste manifests.

#### Recycling & Sorting
Onsite or offsite separation of recyclable waste streams including paper, plastic,
glass, and metal — reducing landfill dependency and supporting your organisation's
sustainability goals.

#### Hazardous Waste Management
Secure handling, treatment, and disposal of toxic, medical, and chemical wastes in
full compliance with the National Environmental Management: Waste Act (NEM:WA) and
relevant SANS standards.

#### Organic Waste & Composting
Processing food and garden waste into compost or animal feed, diverting organic
material from landfill and creating value from waste.

#### Landfill Management
Planning, construction, and operation of compliant landfill sites for non-recyclable
waste, including liner systems, leachate management, and gas capture.

**Compliance note:** All waste management services are carried out in compliance with
the National Environmental Management: Waste Act (NEM:WA, Act 59 of 2008) and
applicable municipal by-laws.
