## Section 3 — Our Story

**Section label:** Our Story

**Heading:** A Decade of Building Excellence

**Body copy:**
> Phehlwana Group Investments was founded in 2015 with a clear purpose: to provide
> high-quality, reliable construction and engineering services to clients who deserved
> better than the status quo. Starting with a small but dedicated team, the company
> quickly earned a reputation for professionalism, attention to detail, and getting
> the job done right.
>
> In the years that followed, Phehlwana Group expanded its service offering to include
> mechanical engineering, cleaning and waste management, and plant hire — becoming a
> true one-stop solution for clients across the construction and facilities management
> sectors. Each new service area was built on the same foundation: experienced people,
> quality execution, and a genuine commitment to client success.
>
> Today, with over 129 completed projects, 50+ skilled professionals, and a growing
> footprint across Gauteng, Phehlwana Group Investments continues to grow — driven by
