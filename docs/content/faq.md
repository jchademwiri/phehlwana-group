**Q: What information do I need to provide for a quote?**

A: The more detail you can provide, the more accurate your quote will be. Helpful
information includes:
- Project location and site address
- Scope of work — what needs to be done
- Preferred start date and project timeline
- Any relevant drawings, plans, or specifications
- Your budget range (if known)

For plant hire, also include the type of equipment needed, hire duration, and
whether you need an operator.

---

**Q: How long does it take to receive a quote?**

A: We aim to provide quotes within 1–2 business days for standard projects. For
larger or more complex projects that require a site visit, we will confirm a
timeline when we acknowledge your request.

---

**Q: Are your quotes free?**

A: Yes, all quotes are free and carry no obligation.

---

**Q: Do you have hidden costs?**

A: No. Our quotes are detailed and itemised so you know exactly what you're paying
for. If anything changes during the project that affects the cost, we will discuss
it with you before proceeding.

**Q: What is the difference between wet hire and dry hire?**

A: **Wet hire** means the equipment comes with a qualified operator provided by us.
**Dry hire** means you hire the equipment only and provide your own operator.

Wet hire is recommended if you don't have a qualified operator, or if you want us
to take responsibility for the safe operation of the equipment. See our
[blog post on wet hire vs dry hire](/blog) for a full comparison.

## Category: Cleaning and Waste Management

---

**Q: Do you offer once-off cleaning or only ongoing contracts?**

A: We offer both. We can provide once-off deep cleans, post-construction cleans,
and event cleaning, as well as ongoing daily or weekly contract cleaning services.

---

**Q: Are you compliant with NEM:WA for waste management?**

A: Yes. All our waste management services are carried out in full compliance with
the National Environmental Management: Waste Act (NEM:WA, Act 59 of 2008). We
provide proper waste manifests for all hazardous waste transport and disposal.

---

**Q: Can you handle hazardous waste?**

A: Yes. We are equipped to handle hazardous waste including chemical waste, asbestos,
and contaminated materials. All hazardous waste is managed by trained personnel
with the correct PPE and disposed of at licensed facilities.

---

**Q: Do you provide cleaning staff or cleaning equipment?**

A: We provide both — our cleaning teams arrive fully equipped with all necessary
cleaning materials and equipment. You don't need to supply anything.

## Category: Safety and Compliance

---

**Q: Are you OHS Act compliant?**

A: Yes. We operate in full compliance with the Occupational Health and Safety Act
(OHS Act 85 of 1993) and the Construction Regulations (2014). Every project has
a dedicated safety officer, documented risk assessments, and daily toolbox talks.

---

**Q: Do you provide PPE for your workers?**

A: Yes. We provide all required personal protective equipment (PPE) to our workers
at no cost to them. This includes hard hats, high-visibility vests, steel-toed
boots, gloves, and safety glasses as a minimum.
