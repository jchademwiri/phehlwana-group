## Bio Writing Guidelines

Each bio should be 3–5 sentences and cover:

1. **Years of experience** in their field
2. **Qualifications** — degree, diploma, or trade certificate (one or two, not an exhaustive list)
3. **Key areas of expertise** relevant to their role at Phehlwana Group
4. **What they bring to the team** — a personal touch or professional passion

**Example bio — Construction Manager:**
> "Sipho has over 15 years of experience in the South African construction industry,
> specialising in commercial and residential builds across Gauteng. He holds a National
> Diploma in Civil Engineering and has managed projects valued at over R50 million.
> Sipho is passionate about mentoring young tradespeople and ensuring every project is
> delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards."

**Example bio — Safety Officer:**
> "Nomsa is a qualified SHEQ practitioner with 10 years of experience in construction
> and industrial safety management. She holds a SAMTRAC certificate and is registered
> with SACPCMP. Nomsa leads our safety culture at Phehlwana Group, ensuring every site
> operates in full compliance with the OHS Act and that every worker goes home safely."

**Example bio — Managing Director:**
> "As the founder and Managing Director of Phehlwana Group Investments, [Name] has
> built the company from the ground up since 2015. With a background in [field] and
> over [X] years of industry experience, [he/she] leads the company's strategic
> direction and maintains the high standards of quality and integrity that define
> the Phehlwana Group brand."

## Photo Guidelines

Consistent, professional headshots make a significant difference to the credibility
of the team page. Please follow these guidelines:

- **Resolution:** Minimum 600x600px (square crop preferred)
- **Background:** Neutral — white, light grey, or a branded colour
- **Attire:** Professional — branded workwear or business casual
- **Expression:** Approachable and confident — a natural smile works well
- **Lighting:** Natural or studio lighting; avoid harsh shadows or backlighting
- **Format:** JPG or WebP, optimised for web (under 200KB per image)

> **Recommendation:** Book a single photography session for all team members on the
> same day to ensure visual consistency across the page.
