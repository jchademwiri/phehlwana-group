Wet hire of a full earthmoving fleet for bulk earthworks on a new residential estate development in Centurion. Phehlwana Group supplied TLBs, excavators, tipper trucks, and a water cart with experienced operators for the duration of the earthworks phase.

## Equipment Supplied

The following plant was supplied on a wet hire basis for this project:

- **2× TLB (Tractor Loader Backhoe)** — bulk excavation, trench digging for services, and material loading
- **1× 20-tonne tracked excavator** — bulk cut and fill earthworks
- **3× tipper trucks** — spoil removal and imported fill material delivery
- **1× water cart** — dust suppression across the 14-hectare site

All equipment was operated by Phehlwana Group's own operators, who were inducted onto the site and worked under the developer's principal contractor.

## Project Details

The earthworks phase involved:

- Bulk cut and fill to achieve design platform levels across the full site
- Excavation of stormwater retention ponds and drainage channels
- Trenching for water, sewer, and electrical services reticulation
- Compaction of fill layers to geotechnical specification
- Dust suppression throughout to comply with the site's environmental management plan

## Delivery and Logistics

Equipment was mobilised to site within 48 hours of contract award. Phehlwana Group managed all equipment maintenance, operator welfare, and daily pre-use inspections throughout the hire period. The earthworks phase was completed two days ahead of the developer's programme, allowing the civil contractor to commence services installation on schedule.
